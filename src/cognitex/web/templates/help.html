Cognitex Help

What's New: Proactive Learning (v2.0)

Cognitex now learns from your behavior to become more helpful over time:

Email Style Learning

  • Learns your voice - Analyzes sent emails to understand your writing style (tone, formality, greetings, closings)
  • Per-recipient profiles - Learns how you write to different people
  • Draft improvement - Applies learned style to future email drafts
  • Edit tracking - Learns from edits you make to drafts

Response Pattern Learning

  • Tracks decisions - Learns which emails you respond to vs skip
  • Sender patterns - Understands your response rate per sender/domain
  • Intent patterns - Learns which types of emails (questions, FYIs, etc.) need responses
  • Smarter drafting - Eventually stops drafting for emails you never respond to

How to Help It Learn

Action What It Learns
Send emails Your writing style (greeting, closing, tone, length, common phrases)
Skip inbox items Which emails don't need responses (use the Skip button on email drafts)
Edit drafts before sending How to improve future drafts (significant edits update your style profile)
Reject drafts That a draft was unhelpful (tracked as discarded)
Approve drafts as-is That the style was correct (reinforces current patterns)
Tip: The more emails you send through the system, the better it learns your style. Check the Learning page to see what patterns have been detected.
